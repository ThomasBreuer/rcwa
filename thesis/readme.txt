
Contents of this directory:

thesis.pdf: The author's thesis `Restklassenweise affine Gruppen', officially
            published at http://elib.uni-stuttgart.de/opus/volltexte/2005/2448/.

thesis_e.pdf: An english translation of that thesis.

              The numbers of theorems and pages in the translation are the same
              as in the original version. Thus if you cite e.g. Theorem 2.12.8
              or refer to page 64, the target is the same regardless of whether
              you use the original or the translation.
