
Contents of this directory:

thesis.pdf: The author's thesis `Restklassenweise affine Gruppen'.

            All errata known on release date are corrected in this copy.
            A list of errata is maintained on the author's homepage.

thesis_e.pdf: An english translation of the author's thesis
              `Restklassenweise affine Gruppen'.

              The numbers of theorems and pages in the translation are the same
              as in the original version. Thus if you cite e.g. Theorem 2.12.8
              or refer to page 64, the target is the same regardless of whether
              you use the original or the translation.

              All errata known on release date are corrected in this copy.
              Typographical errors in the translation are corrected in
              the copy distributed via the RCWA package and via the author's
              homepage as they are found.
